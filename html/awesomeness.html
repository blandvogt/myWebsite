Books

Sprint - Jake Knapp

Knapp provides an innovative 5 day process for developing and testing new prototypes in a Lean Development Framework.

  1. Monday: Discover Product Issues
  2. Tuesday: Individule Brainstorming
  3. Wednesday: Plan Prototype For Best Idea
  4. Thuresday: Build Prototype
  5. Friday: Test Prototype

Data Science for Business - Foster Provost, Tom Fawcett

This is authored by the Prof. of Business Analytics at the NYU Stern School of Management. It's a great way for business minded people to learn how analytics can influence decisions.

Every standard deviation greater that a firm implements Data-Driven Decision Making, is correlated to a 4-6% increase in productivity.
